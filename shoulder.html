Shoulder Exercises

Military Press (Overhead Press)

Description: Stand or sit with a barbell or dumbbells at shoulder height, press the weight overhead until arms are fully extended, then lower it back down to shoulder height.

Sets and Reps: Start with 3 sets of 8-12 reps.

Injury Prevention: Keep your core engaged for stability, avoid overarching the lower back, and maintain a slight bend in the knees to prevent strain.

Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Description: Sit or stand with dumbbells at shoulder height, press the weights overhead until arms are fully extended, then lower them back down to shoulder height.

Sets and Reps: Aim for 3 sets of 8-12 reps.

Injury Prevention: Maintain proper posture with a neutral spine, avoid shrugging your shoulders, and control the weights throughout the movement to prevent jerking.

Lateral Raises

Description: Hold dumbbells at your sides with palms facing inward, raise the weights out to the sides until arms are parallel to the ground, then lower them back down.

Sets and Reps: Perform 3 sets of 10-15 reps.

Injury Prevention: Use light to moderate weights, keep a slight bend in the elbows, and avoid swinging the weights to maintain control.

Front Raises

Description: Hold dumbbells with palms facing down, raise the weights in front of you until arms are parallel to the ground, then lower them back down.

Sets and Reps: Start with 3 sets of 10-12 reps.

Injury Prevention: Engage your core for stability, avoid using momentum to lift the weights, and keep a slight bend in the elbows throughout the movement.

Rear Delt Flyes

Description: Bend forward at the hips with dumbbells in hand, arms hanging straight down, raise the weights out to the sides until arms are parallel to the ground, then lower them back down.

Sets and Reps: Aim for 3 sets of 10-12 reps.

Injury Prevention: Keep your back flat and neck in a neutral position, avoid rounding the shoulders forward, and use controlled movements to prevent strain on the shoulder joints.
